## Summary

Amanmeet Garg is an Applied Scientist and a leader with 12 years of experience (startups, Nielsen Media, Amazon) shipping CV/ML products and deep research. Aman's breadth of experience include Deep Learning based CV (Amazon Prime Video, Nielsen Media), Classical computer vision (MDAcne, LUUM robotics) and modern Deep Learning techniques such as Large Language Models, and CV foundation models. He has built Agentic AI automation systems at global scale in Amazon Prime Video, deep tech computer vision deployed on iOS mobile device, and Media Foundation Model at Amazon. His work has lead to 22 papers, 7 patents, 1200+ citations and delivered impact touching 200 Million customers across 180 countries generating in Billion dollar scale business value.

Aman completed his PhD work in high dimensional topological data analysis in neuroimaging biomarkers (2017) and MS work in statistical signal processing with wavelet transforms for human fall prevention (2011) at Simon Fraser University, Canada.

His current research interests are in building spatial intelligence with Foundational Models and Generative AI and working on world models and vision language action model.


## Research Statement

My research is driven by the question: *How can we build neural networks with spatial intelligence aligned with human intelligence?* The joint training for video generation and understanding, and physics and causality grounded world models and vision-language-action models is my current line of inquiry.
Second, *How can we build small multimodal foundation models with extreme task specific performance?* World is inherently multi-modal, and co-interacts across all modalities at all times in different combination patterns. A large multi-modal foundation model is performant, though poses many challenges to scale. Here, I aim to build methods to create smaller models which can be widely deployed to the masses and retain all capabilities same as the large multi-modal foundation models.
